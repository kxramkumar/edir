Summary table
Step	Preferred approach	Alternative	Reason
Size	CV2 interpolation (LANCZOS4)	Real-ESRGAN	Size step only needs consistent dimensions; interpolation is fast and good enough.
Blur	Unsharp mask / OpenCV	Real-ESRGAN	Fast and effective for 2k+ images; ESRGAN for small tests only.
Dark	CLAHE (gray or per-channel)	Gamma / normalize	CLAHE is the standard for medical/dark regions; your formula is appropriate.
So: use CV2 for size, OpenCV sharpening for blur, and CLAHE for dark as the main pipeline; treat Real-ESRGAN as optional for size/blur when you’re willing to pay the cost (time/GPU).
